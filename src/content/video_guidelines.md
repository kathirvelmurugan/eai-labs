# Video Content Guidelines (v1.0)

## Brand Rules
- **Text**: lowercase only (non-negotiable).
- **Tone**: Calm, premium, systems-first. No hype.
- **Visual**: Black background, white text/lines. No gradients.
- **Logo**: Aircraft on top, brand below. Do not animate aggressively.
- **Sound**: Minimal, ambient, neutral. Silence is acceptable.

## Standard Flow
1. **Scene 1**: One uncomfortable truth (problem). quiet black screen.
2. **Scene 2**: Short pause or subtle motion.
3. **Scene 3**: Clear statement of the gap.
4. **Scene 4**: Calm resolution.
5. **Scene 5**: Brand identity (logo).

## What to Avoid
- Stock footage of happy teams.
- High-energy music.
- Fast cuts.
- Emojis.
